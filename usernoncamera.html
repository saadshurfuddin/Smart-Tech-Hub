Non-camera E-surveillance solutions

BelBird Technologies brings a unique non-camera-based surveillance solution to protect homes and apartment complexes. Our solution is based on sensors and proprietary software applications running on servers and mobile devices. Unlike a camera-based solution, no discomfort and privacy risk, is hidden so cannot be broken, raise an alarm in real time, 30-50% less expensive and simple installation.

  1. BelBird e-Surveillance system adds surveillance capability to spaces without using the camera.
  2. Surveillance of spaces to identify intruders without affecting the privacy of legitimate occupants.
  3. In addition, it addresses the care needs of elders/children etc.
