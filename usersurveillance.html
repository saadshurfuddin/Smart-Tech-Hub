BelBird M-Lock

We at BelBird Technologies bring security together with keyless convenience to the homeowner through BelBird m-Lock on their mobile phones. BelBird m-Locks are hassle-free to use, provide ease of mind, convenient to make copies of software keys, and add & configure dependent users. Access can be extended to another person and access is flexible as can be configured i.e. for a full-time, limited period, or one time.

  • Mobile app operated
  • Auto Lock
  • Digital Safety Door Lock
  • Hands-free lock operation
  • Buzzer alarm when door is left open
  • Works in Router and Router-Less mode
